How we account

Accounting methods we apply

Beyond day-to-day bookkeeping, we bring the right accounting treatment to your numbers, so your statements hold up to accountants, lenders, and investors.

Cash & accrual basis

The right basis for your business, and clean support if you need to switch.

FIFO & weighted-average costing

Inventory valued the way that best reflects how your product actually moves.

Landed cost accounting

Freight, duty, and import costs rolled into true unit cost for product businesses.

Job & project costing

Costs tracked by job or project so you see margin where it is earned.

Revenue recognition

Deferred and subscription revenue recognized in the correct period.

Trade spend & deductions

Chargebacks and allowances recorded and disputed, not silently absorbed.

Accruals & prepaids

Expenses and prepaid schedules matched to the periods they belong to.

Fixed assets & depreciation

Asset registers and depreciation schedules kept current and accurate.

13-week cash-flow forecasting

Forward visibility on cash for planning and lending conversations.
