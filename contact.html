Common questions

Frequently asked questions

What do you need from me to get started?

Read-only access to your accounting file (such as QuickBooks Online or Xero) and your recent bank and credit card statements. From there we assess where the books stand and propose a plan.

How does pricing work?

Pricing depends on transaction volume, the number of accounts, and the services you need. After a short review of your books we provide a clear monthly quote before any work begins.

Which accounting software do you support?

QuickBooks Online is our primary platform, and we also work with Xero, Sage Intacct, and the payment, inventory, and reporting tools listed on our Technology page.

How do we share documents securely?

Through secure shared folders, direct bank feeds, and receipt capture tools such as Dext or HubDoc. We never ask for your banking passwords.

When will I receive my monthly reports?

On a timeline we agree together during onboarding. Each month you receive your Profit and Loss, Balance Sheet, and any client-specific reports once every account is reconciled and reviewed.

Do you replace my CPA or tax preparer?

No. We keep your books clean, reconciled, and ready so your CPA can file faster. We are happy to work alongside your existing tax professional.
